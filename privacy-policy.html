Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Vinyl Collector ("the App"), developed by Filipe Tavares, handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Collect

Vinyl Collector is designed to work entirely offline. All data you enter — including your vinyl record collection, ratings, notes, and wishlist — is stored locally on your device only using Apple's standard storage APIs (AsyncStorage). We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

2. Discogs API (Optional Feature)

The App optionally connects to the Discogs public API to search for album information and retrieve market prices. This connection is initiated only when you explicitly perform a search or request a price update. No personal information is sent to Discogs — only the search query you type (e.g., album title or barcode). We do not require a Discogs account and do not store any Discogs credentials.

Discogs has its own privacy policy, available at: https://www.discogs.com/privacy

3. Camera and Photo Library

The App may request access to your camera (to scan vinyl barcodes) and your photo library (to add cover images to records). These permissions are requested only when you initiate the relevant action and are never used for any other purpose. No photos or camera data are transmitted outside your device.

4. Data Storage and Security

All your collection data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data. If you delete the App, all locally stored data will be permanently removed. We recommend using the built-in export feature (CSV/JSON) to back up your collection before uninstalling.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. The only external service used is the Discogs public API, as described in Section 2.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App does not collect any personal information from any user.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
